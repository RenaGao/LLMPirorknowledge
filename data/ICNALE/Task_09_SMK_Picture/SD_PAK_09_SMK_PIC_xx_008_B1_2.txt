
A few weeks ago, a mother and his child went to a playground where the child was playing with the sand.  There was a stranger standing nearby who was smoking a cigarette in public place.  The mother of the child saw that man smoking and because her child was being affected with the smoke.  The mother went up to the stranger and said, "you cannot smoke here because it is affecting the health of my child and as well as yours".  The man, the total stranger got affected by the words of the mother and put off his cigarette.  At the end, the man apologized for smoking in to the public place and apologized.
